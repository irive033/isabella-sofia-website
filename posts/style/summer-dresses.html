Summer Dresses

Isabella Rivera · Apr 15, 2026

A curated guide to sustainable summer dresses from eco-conscious fashion brands, emphasizing natural, breathable fabrics like cotton, linen, and hemp.

At a Glance

1–5 / 7

SIR

A great mini dress should feel as good as it looks, and natural fibers like silk are the secret to that effortless, lived-in luxury. It drapes better, breathes beautifully, and ages with grace. What I love about SIR.'s Dabica Mini Dress is that the bias-cut silk-chiffon delivers that liquid, sun-catching quality you only get from the real thing, while the bright orange hue and layered hem keep it feeling fresh. 

Dabica Layered Silk Mini, $520

Attire the Studio

Summer dressing is all about fabrics that breathe, and a knitted linen-cotton blend is the kind of effortless choice that takes you from beach club to dinner without ever feeling overworked. In addition to their transparency, Attire the Studio's commitment to being plastic-free makes them a go-to for all occasions. Every fabric is 100% natural and biodegradable, the entire collection is plastic-free (down to the threads and zippers), and production happens in small, family-run factories in Portugal and Italy with full cost breakdowns published for each piece.

Lala Dress, $360

Reformation

There's something about a long silk dress that earns its keep season after season, and 100% silk charmeuse is what separates a dress that feels expensive from one that just looks it. That plus the romantic detailing on Reformations Lilibeth dress make it perfect for Summer. A cowl neckline, asymmetrical hem, and detachable sheer cape takes the slip silhouette somewhere a little more interesting, especially for weddings, dinners, and date nights. 

Lilibeth Silk Dress, $328

Colleen Allen

Every wardrobe deserves at least one true investment piece, and a lingerie-inspired silk gown in lustrous 100% silk-satin is exactly the kind of dress that justifies the splurge. What I love about Colleen Allen's design is the lace detailing along the neckline and the way the slip-style cami contours the body before falling into a fluid floor-length skirt. It's intimate without trying too hard. 

Lace-Trimmed Silk Gown, $2,950
