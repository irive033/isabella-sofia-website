Morgan Stewart's Capsule Wardrobe

Isabella Rivera · May 7, 2026

I was recently listening to Morgan Stewart's latest episode of her new podcast (which is everything I've been wanting and needing - a must listen if you haven't yet) and she very graciously did a quick run through of a Morgan Stewart approved capsule wardrobe, so I was of course immediately inspired and needed to put pen to paper. 

And in the spirit of the 'why' behind her outlining this capsule wardbrobe for us, everything is under $500.

Black Pants *that are flattering*

The *that are flattering* part is key. A good fitting pant vs a bad one will make or break your look, but once you find the style that you like and likes you, the combinations are endless. I love Reformations Alex pant because it gives you a little bit of everything. A universally flattering mid-rise, linen fabric that will take you from season to season, and an effortlessly relaxed fit throughout. Wear it running errands or to a date night, from daytime meetings to evening events.

Reformation Alex Linen Mid-Rise Pant$218

A White Tee

What would this Morgan Stewart-inspired capsule wardrobe be without one of her thoughtful and timeless pieces from Renggli? While you may not think of a t-shirt as necessarily chic, the cropped fit and attention to detail give an otherwise forgettable piece exactly what it needs to be versatile and covetable. It can by styled up or down depending on where you're heading and what the vibe is, but either way you'll having people stopping you to ask where you got your top.

Renggli Crop Tee$120

A Black Tee

And of course if you're getting it in white, you must also get it in black. Between the two colors you'll have an infinite number of way to style them. White for day, black for night. Summer, Spring, Fall, and Winter, you'll be reaching for this classic black tee time and time again. Renggli says it best -  perfection in simplicity, 100% cotton, goes with everything and looks great doing it. A true wardrobe foundation.

Renggli Crop Tee$120

Black Sweater

My interpretation of a sweater is a cardigan because I love how truly versatile they are. Wear it on its on as a top, layer it over one of the Renggli Tees, or tie it over your shoulders for something that's both functional and pulls your look together. LilySilk has no shortage of great options, but I particularly like the fitted cardigan for an ultra flattering and form fitting look. 

LilySilk Fitted Cardigan$139

Beige Sweater

To me, no one does cashmere and cropped cardigans better than N.Peal. And their Superfine line is exactly that, buttery soft and lightweight. Its the perfect weight for every season and the Darcie Cropped Cardigan is my absolute go-to. The cropped fit and 3/4 sleeves create a refined and feminine look while still being casual enough to layer and wear for all occasions.

N.Peal Superfine Cropped Cardigan$365

Black Blazer

A black blazer is a must in any capsule wardrobe. It can instantly elevate the most basic outfit and add just the right amount of corporate professional chic to any look. While most blazers around the $500 price are made from synthetic fabrics like acetate and polyester, I would opt for linen or even wool if you live in a year-round colder climate. What I love about Massimo Dutti's Linen Blazer is that it offers the year-round wearability of linen with the elevated tailoring necessary for more formal events.

Massimo Dutti Linen Blazer$320

*Really Good Fitted* Dark Denim Pants/Jean

Again the *really good fitted* is a must for your dark denim jeans. Just like with the black pants, a pair of ill-fitted jeans can ruin the entire look. While your really good fitted style will be personal to you, I'm loving the versatility and generally universally flattering mid/high-rise flare jean look right now. Agolde is a great go-to for all things denim, and the Leena jean is just as perfect for daytime lunch meetings as it is for date nights.

Agolde Leena Flare Jeans$345

& A Light Pair for Summer

I think a light wash pair of denim is incredibly underrated so I was so glad to hear it included in Morgans list of wardrobe staples. They're perfect for the Spring and Summer months and just like all the other pieces can be dressed up or down. Going to a playdate in the park? Pair them with the Renggli black tee and Repetto ballet flats. Headed to Friday night drinks and a concert? Keep the tee and swap the flats for Staud's Brigitte heel.

Rohe Wide-Leg Jean$420

A Pair of Shorts

While a pair of shorts can mean many things, the way a denim short can be styled make it the ultimate capsule wardrobe staple. From pool days to nights out, there is no shortage of combinations, and its acceptable is mostly every setting. Re/Done is the epitome of denim and the A Frame short is the perfect style that will last the test of time. Pair it with a tee and flats for a daytime look and a blazer and heels for the evening, it never stops serving.

Re/Done A Frame Short$205

Honorable Mention to a Ballet Flat

She didn't cover shoes, but I know these would be approved.

We can't close out the capsule wardrobe collection without at least adding in a couple of shoes. Morgan herself has talked about the important of a good ballet flat, so while she didn't mention it, I know she'd approve. There's many great options within the $500 price range, but the history and craftsmanship of Repetto makes their classic Cendrillon Ballet Flat a top choice. Created at the request of Bridgette Bardot, the shoe is just as elegant and feminine as it is practical.

Repetto Cendrillon Ballet Flat$425

& A Heel

The last piece to complete your capsule wardrobe is undoubtably a heel. Without one, it's hard to elevate a look to a truly more formal realm. What I love most about Staud's Brigitte, aside from the very reasonable 3.15in heel, is the slingback design which makes it not only more comfortable but infinitely more wearable. The satin fabric certainly reads more evening wear, but because it's in a neutral black color, you can still get away with wearing it during the daytime by pairing it with Renggli's crop white tee, Re/Done's A Frame short, one of the cardigans tied over your shoulders.

Staud Brigitte Heel$375

And there's your easy, simplified, and endlessly versatile capsule wardrobe to take you through all seasons, events, and activities.
