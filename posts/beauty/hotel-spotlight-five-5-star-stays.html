Hotel Spotlight: Five 5-Star Stays

Isabella Rivera · Apr 15, 2026

A luxury travel itinerary spanning five prestigious hotels across five months, each chosen for distinct characteristics and seasonal appropriateness.

I. 1 Hotel Central Park, NYC (May)

  • Environmentally conscious, great location, friendly service. 
  • Located steps from Central Park, this is the perfect base for summer exploration in the city.

Recognition: 1 Michelin Key

II. Bvlgari Hotel, Milan (June)

[Image placeholder: Bvlgari Hotel Milan photo]

Sleek and refined, quiet yet centrally located with friendly service. Positioned near shopping and historic landmarks, this is the ultimate European luxury stay.

Recognition: 2 Michelin Keys

III. Four Seasons Resort & Residences, Puerto Rico (July)

[Image placeholder: Four Seasons Puerto Rico photo]

Private and secluded, beachfront, large property. A tropical vacation atmosphere that offers the perfect summer escape.

IV. Badrutt's Palace, St. Moritz (August)

[Image placeholder: Badrutt's Palace photo]

The standard for 5-star service and luxury stays. A destination that defines what it means to experience true hospitality.

Recognition: 3 Michelin Keys

V. Waldorf Astoria, NYC (September)

Art-Deco, historic, grandeur. Welcome the autumn season in New York at one of the most iconic hotels in the world.
