Everyday Hair Routine

Isabella Rivera · May 7, 2026

Simple, effective hair care for everyday styling. A routine that keeps your hair healthy and looking it's best, without taking hours.

Finding the right balance between washing, conditioning, and styling can transform your hair. These are the steps and products that work for maintaining healthy, manageable hair day to day.

Step 1: Treatment or Deep Cleanse

Just like with your skincare, a double cleanse can transform how your hair looks, feels, and grows. It"s important to pay attention to your hair type and needs, but if you find yourself using lots of products post shower, having an oily scalp, or waiting a few days in between washes, a treatment pre shower or double cleanse may just be the thing to get you your dream hair. Whether it's glycolic acid massaged into your scalp pre shower, a ketoconazale shampoo, or a clariyfing shampoo, try adding in a product to specifically target oils, product build up, or any other concerns you notice with your hair. 

Step 2: Target Cleanse

For your second cleanse, you can pick something that targets how you want your hair to look and feel post shower. Think volume, shine, smoothing, etc. What's also great about incorporating two cleanses into your routine is that you can alternate using only one on days your hair doesn't need a full detox. 

Step 3: Condition

When it comes to conditioners, knowing your hair type and needs is of the utmost importance. Choose the wrong one and your hair could be weighed down and leave you looking like you need to go right back into the shower; or on the other side, could leave it feeling and looking stringy, crunchy, and frizzy. For maximum results and if you like to stick with the same brand, look for the complimenting conditioner to your shampoo. 

Step 4: Style & Protect

Last but certainly not least in your routine should be a style and protect product. This can be an all in one or you may find that two different products - one for styling, and one for protecting - works best for you. Either way, you should look for something that you can apply after towel drying your hair and before applying any heat products. Pay close attention to the instructions as some products, like the ColorWow Dream Coat and K18 Leave-In Mask, require you to follow specific steps in order for them to work.
